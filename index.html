About

I drove a box truck for the Inter-Faith Food Shuttle for 4 years after high school. About two years in, I took a few programming classes at Wake Tech. These classes turned the proverbial light bulb on and inspired me to pursue an education and career in software and web development.

Education

I graduated from The Coding Bootcamp at UNC-Chapel Hill in 2018. I'm slowly but surely working part time towards a CS degree. If you ask me when I'm going to graduate my response will be "forever and a day."

Toolbox

Most of my experience is with JavaScript, HTML, CSS, React, and Node.js. More recently I've had the opportunity to work with C in the embedded software arena. I've also had some academic exposure to Python, Java, C#, MySQL and MongoDB.

MIDA Medical

I joined MIDA in 2018 two weeks after I graduated from the code bootcamp at UNC. I accomplished this goal within three months and spent my remaing time there documenting my work, developing a webapp to help with database management, leading weekly intern review sessions, and contributing to some frontend development.
